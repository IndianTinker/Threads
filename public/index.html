Welcome to "Threads" - an interactive installation by Rohit at function().

How

Click on the 'Play' link on the top. You will then be connected with the function() location.
As you move your finger across the screen, interesting patterns emerge to amuse you. They in-turn affect the installation at the gallery by altering the space there. This introduces you in mediated connection with the gallery. I use this "mediated connection" as a metaphor for the communications we have today.

You should take screenshots of any interesting patterns to find. :)

What

In today’s digitally submersed life, human connections and perceptions have been mediated to an extent that it is challenging the subjective uniqueness of an individual. We seem to be so busy doing nothing but communicating.
We are surrounded, trapped, and amused by these multitude of 'threads' around us.
